A place to remember

Welcome to Malshej Lake Camping, where you can experience the beauty of nature in the serene surroundings of the Sahyadri mountains. Our camping site is located near Malshej Ghat in Maharashtra, India, and offers the perfect retreat for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

At Malshej Lake Camping, we provide a comfortable and safe camping experience for individuals, families, and groups. Our tents are spacious and equipped with all the necessary amenities. We also have clean and hygienic restroom facilities for our guests.

One of the highlights of our camping site is the picturesque Malshej Lake or backwaters of pimpalgao joga dam, which is located infront of the campsite. Guests can enjoy a leisurely swim in the crystal clear waters or go boating. The surrounding hills and forests offer a perfect setting for hiking and trekking.
